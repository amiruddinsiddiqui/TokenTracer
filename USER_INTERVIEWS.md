## Interview 1 — Sam, ML Intern at YMinds.AI
Company stage: AI startup

I talked with Sam about how his team uses AI tools during development and whether they think much about subscription costs.

Some quotes from the conversation:
- “The startup probably pays for more AI tools than we actually use.”
- “Most people just buy whatever tool helps them code better and faster.”

The most surprising thing was that some team members were paying for both Cursor and ChatGPT even though they mostly relied on one daily.

This conversation made me add clearer savings breakdowns because sam said pricing discussions usually happen casually in team chats instead of formal budgeting meetings.
